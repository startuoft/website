Space Travel Analog Research Team (START)

The Team

START is a team of University of Toronto students participating in the 2024/2025 Canadian Stratospheric Balloon Experiment Design Challenge.

This Year’s Mission


As NASA’s Gateway project works to return humanity to the moon and new engineering solutions are crucial for success. Without Earth’s atmosphere to protect astronauts, space radiation becomes a major threat to their health and future space exploration.

Fortunately, the stratospheric environment serves as an ideal Earth-based analog to space radiation. Our design aims to expose human cells to this radiation while keeping them alive, offering a unique opportunity for research on the impact that Galactic Cosmic Rays have on human DNA.

Our design aims to expose human cells to radiation at high altitudes, offering research opportunities on Galactic Cosmic Rays.

Includes gas-tight trays to maintain pressure and a temperature control chamber set at 37°C.
